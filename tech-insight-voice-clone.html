Meta’s AI Can Clone Your Voice in Another Language

Aug 20, 2025

Meta has rolled out an AI‑powered voice translation tool for Instagram and Facebook creators. It can dub videos into other languages while preserving the speaker’s tone and lip movements. Initially supporting English ↔ Spanish, the feature aims to help creators reach global audiences without losing authenticity. Creators can review and tweak translations before publishing, and new analytics will show viewership by language. More languages are expected in future updates..

How it works

Meta’s AI-powered tool uses advanced voice synthesis and translation models to automatically dub Reels into another language — currently English ↔ Spanish. What makes it unique is:

  • Voice cloning: It mimics the creator’s actual voice, tone, and delivery instead of using a generic narrator.
  • Lip sync: Optional feature aligns translated audio with the speaker’s mouth movements for realism.
  • Creator control: Users can preview, approve, or disable translations before publishing.
  • Analytics: New metrics show how translated content performs across languages.

Use cases

  • Accessibility: Makes content understandable for non-native speakers and hearing-impaired users.
  • Localization: Helps creators grow audiences in new regions without re-recording.
  • Content creation: Enables multilingual storytelling with minimal effort.
